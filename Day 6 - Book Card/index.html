Dune

Novel by Frank Herbert

Set in the far future amidst a sprawling feudal interstellar empire where planetary dynasties are controlled by noble houses that owe an allegiance to the imperial House Corrino, Dune tells the story of young Paul Atreides (the heir apparent to Duke Leto Atreides and heir of House Atreides) as he and his family accept control of the desert planet Arrakis...

Orginally Published: 1965
Author: Frank Herbert
Followed by: Dune Messiah
Page Counts: 412
Adaptations: Dune(1984), Frank Herbert's Dune (2000)
Characters: Paul Atreides, Vladimir Harkonnen, Chani, Leto Atreides I, Lady Jessica, Princess Irulan, Alia Atreides, Duncan Idaho, Feyd-Rautha, Thufir Hawat, Gurney Halleck, Shaddam IV Liet-Kynes, Piter De Vries, Gaius Helen Mohiam, Gloss Rabban Hasimir Fenring
